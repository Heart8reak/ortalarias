About Ortal Arias

Ortal was born and raised in Jerusalem, Israel. Since a very young age, she has learned how to dance and act on stage and today she is pursuing her dream of becoming an established actress in NYC. Ortal is a graduate of 4 years of Theater major in high-school and served 2 years in the Israeli Air-Force. She irradiates full of positive energy that she passes to everybody the minute she meet them and strongly believes that making other people happy and help them grow will make you happier and better your life. In her free time, Ortal loves to meditate, workout, read books, spend time with her husband and family, and dance salsa.
